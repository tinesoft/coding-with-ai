# **Learning Approach**

## **Human-in-the-Loop Methodology**
<br>

### **AI as Assistant, Not Replacement**

- **You lead**: Define problems, validate solutions
- **AI accelerates**: Suggests fixes, patterns, improvements
- **You decide**: Accept, reject, or modify AI suggestions

### **Critical Thinking Required**
Every AI suggestion must be verified and understood

Notes:
This approach builds competency and prevents over-reliance on AI tools. You'll learn when to trust AI and when to question it.
